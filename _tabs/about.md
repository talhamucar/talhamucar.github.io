# About Me

Hi, I'm **Talha Ucar**; a technology enthusiast with a strong focus on **CyberSecurity** and a growing interest in **Artificial Intelligence**.

I hold a **Diploma in Computer Science** from **Concordia University** and have completed the **Master of Engineering in Information Systems Security** program at **Concordia University**.

Currently, I work as a **Vulnerability Analyst at CAE**, where I specialize in identifying, assessing, mitigating, and remediating security vulnerabilities across systems and environments. My work is centered on improving security posture, reducing risk, and supporting compliance through practical and effective security measures.

## Professional Focus

My experience includes working with **Microsoft technologies**, particularly:

- **Microsoft technologies such as Azure, Intune, and Defender**
- **Vulnerability remediation and risk reduction**
- **Governance and compliance topics such as CMMC and CIS**

## Areas of Interest

This blog is where I share my thoughts, notes, and learnings on two main areas:

### CyberSecurity

CyberSecurity is one of the main pillars of this blog. I write about vulnerability management, remediation strategies, security hardening, Microsoft security technologies, and governance topics. I am particularly interested in practical approaches to reducing risk, strengthening security baselines, and improving organizational security posture.

### Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is another major focus of this blog. I am interested in how AI is shaping the future of technology, security, and automation. Here, I share insights, ideas, and explorations related to AI tools, emerging trends, and the intersection of AI and CyberSecurity.

## Why This Blog?

I created this blog to document my journey, share technical knowledge, and explore ideas at the intersection of **security**, **technology**, and **innovation**. Whether it's a technical note, a cybersecurity insight, or a reflection on AI, this space serves as both a personal knowledge base and a platform to share useful content with others.
